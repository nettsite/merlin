Alerts & Notifications

Merlin watches for conditions that need your attention and surfaces them in three places: a bell icon in the top navigation, an alert panel on the dashboard, and a brief pop-up toast when something new is detected.

The notification bell

The bell in the top navigation shows a badge with the number of active alerts. Click it to open a dropdown listing each alert with a short description. Clicking an alert takes you to the page where you can deal with it.

The bell checks for new alerts automatically every 20 seconds — you don't need to refresh the page.

The dashboard panel

Active alerts also appear in a panel on the dashboard, so anything outstanding is visible the moment you log in.

What Merlin alerts on

AlertWhen it appearsHow to clear it
Unposted invoices One or more purchase invoices are sitting in Received, Reviewed, or Approved status — processed but not yet posted to your accounts. Review and post (or reject) the waiting invoices under Purchase Invoices. The alert clears itself once nothing is waiting.

More alert types will be added over time — the same bell and panel will show them all.

Alerts are shared, not personal Alerts describe the state of the business, not your personal to-do list. Every user who can view documents sees the same alerts, and when one person resolves the underlying issue, the alert clears for everyone.
