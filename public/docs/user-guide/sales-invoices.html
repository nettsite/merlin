Sales Invoices

Sales invoices are the invoices you send to your clients to request payment. Merlin assigns each one a unique number in the format SINV-2026-00001, handles the VAT calculations, emails the invoice to the client, and tracks payments against it.

Creating an invoice

  1. Go to Sales Invoices.
  2. Click New Invoice.
  3. Select the client. Their payment term and receivable account are filled in automatically from their client settings.
  4. Set the invoice date. The due date is calculated automatically from the payment term.
  5. Add line items — each with a description, quantity, unit price, and VAT rate.
  6. The subtotal, VAT, and total update as you add lines.
  7. Add any notes or payment terms text you want to appear on the invoice.
  8. Save.

Sending an invoice

Once the invoice is saved, click Send to email it to the client. Merlin generates a PDF of the invoice and sends it using the email template you have set up in Billing Settings.

The invoice status changes to Sent once the email has been dispatched.

Recording a payment

When a client pays, click Record Payment on the invoice. Enter the amount paid and the date. Merlin records the payment and updates the balance due on the invoice. If the full amount has been received, the invoice status changes to Paid. Partial payments leave the status as Partially paid.

Invoice statuses

StatusWhat it means
DraftCreated but not yet sent.
SentEmailed to the client.
Partially paidSome payment received, balance outstanding.
PaidFully paid.
VoidedCancelled. Cannot be edited or sent.

Voiding an invoice

If an invoice was created by mistake or needs to be cancelled, use Void. A voided invoice is kept on record but cannot be edited, sent, or paid against. This is a permanent action — use it only when the invoice should not exist.

PDF invoices

Merlin generates a PDF version of the invoice automatically when you send it. The PDF includes your company name, address, tax number, and logo from your company settings, along with the client's billing address, all line items, VAT breakdown, and total. See Settings to set up your company details and logo.

Invoice email template

The email that accompanies the invoice is based on a template you design. To set this up, go to Emails › Templates and create a template, then go to Billing Settings and select it as the invoice email template. If no template is selected, sending an invoice will fail.
