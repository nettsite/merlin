Contacts

The Contacts page is a single directory of every person attached to your suppliers and clients — the people you actually email and phone. It answers "who do I speak to at that company?" without opening each supplier or client record.

What you see

Each row is a person with an active contact assignment: their name, contact details, the supplier or client they belong to, and their role there (for example Accounts or Sales).

The same person can appear more than once — a bookkeeper who handles several of your clients is listed once per client they represent.

Where contacts come from

Contacts are created and edited on the supplier or client record itself, not here. To add or change a contact, open the relevant supplier or client and manage its contacts there. This page is read-only.

Contacts receive invoices and quotes When Merlin emails a sales invoice or quote, it goes to the client's contact. Keeping contact email addresses current here is what keeps your billing reaching the right inbox.
